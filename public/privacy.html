MapTheMovie Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026 • GeoFiction Labs Ltd • hello@geofictionlabs.com

We built MapTheMovie so you can play without creating an account. We collect the minimum data needed to run the game and never sell your information.

1. Who We Are

MapTheMovie is operated by GeoFiction Labs Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). Our registered address is available on request. Contact us at hello@geofictionlabs.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Anonymous session ID — generated automatically when you open the app. No name, email, or password required to play.
  • Your location (GPS) — used in-app only to (a) guide you to the puzzle destination via the compass and (b) confirm you have arrived. We do not store your GPS trail. We store only the coordinates you submit to claim a reward.
  • Puzzle answers you submit — stored to prevent brute-force guessing and to award your reward.
  • Email address — only if you choose to create an account (e.g. to access premium hunts or receive notifications). Used only to send a sign-in link.
  • Push notification subscription token — stored if you grant notification permission, so we can send alerts about nearby hunts or new releases.

3. Why We Process Your Data (Legal Basis)

  • Contract performance — to run the game you asked to play.
  • Legitimate interests — preventing abuse, improving the service, analysing aggregate usage.
  • Consent — for push notifications and marketing emails (you can withdraw at any time).

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Anonymous sessions and puzzle answers: 90 days.
  • Redemption records: 2 years (required for business reporting).
  • Email accounts: until you delete your account.
  • Push subscriptions: until you unsubscribe or revoke permission.

5. Who We Share Data With

We use the following sub-processors:

  • Supabase Inc. — database and authentication (EU region). Privacy policy.
  • Vercel Inc. — hosting and CDN. Privacy policy.
  • Stripe Inc. — payment processing for business subscriptions. Privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share your data with partner businesses beyond what is necessary to issue your in-game voucher (i.e. the business receives a redemption code, not your identity).

6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing)

To exercise any right, email hello@geofictionlabs.com. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your anonymous session ID across visits. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Children

MapTheMovie is intended for players aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via the app. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
