Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We built MapTheMovie so you can play without creating an account. We collect the minimum data needed to run the game and never sell your information.

1. Who We Are

MapTheMovie is operated by GeoFiction Labs Ltd, Gillingham, Kent, United Kingdom. You can contact us at hello@geofictionlabs.com. We are the data controller for personal data processed through this service.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Anonymous session ID - generated automatically by Supabase anonymous authentication when you open the app. No name, email, or password is required to play.
  • GPS location - used only during an active hunt to guide you to the puzzle destination and to confirm you have arrived. We do not store your movement trail. Only the final arrival coordinates are recorded to validate your claim.
  • Hunt completion data - which hunts you have completed and which voucher codes have been issued to you. Used to prevent duplicate claims.
  • Email address (optional) - only if you choose to save your progress or receive notifications about new hunts. Used solely to send a sign-in link. You are not required to provide this to play.
  • Push notification subscription token - stored only if you explicitly grant notification permission, so we can send alerts about nearby hunts.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) - to run the game you asked to play, issue vouchers, and confirm arrivals.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) - preventing abuse, improving the service, and producing aggregate usage analytics. No individual tracking is performed.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) - for push notifications and marketing emails. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us or revoking notification permissions in your browser settings.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the treasure hunt service - guiding you to locations and issuing rewards.
  • To send notifications about new hunts near you, if you have opted in.
  • To produce aggregate, anonymised usage analytics (no individual tracking).
  • To prevent fraudulent or duplicate voucher claims.

5. Data Retention

  • Anonymous session data and puzzle answers: 90 days after your last activity.
  • Redemption and completion records: 2 years (required for business reporting purposes).
  • Email addresses: retained until you request deletion. Requests are fulfilled within 30 days.
  • Push notification tokens: until you unsubscribe or revoke permission in your browser.

6. Third-Party Sub-Processors

We share data only with the following processors, who are contractually bound to GDPR-compliant data handling:

  • Supabase Inc. - database, authentication, and real-time services. Data is stored in the EU region. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel Inc. - hosting and CDN delivery. Vercel Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Participating businesses receive only a voucher redemption event - they do not receive your identity, email address, or location data.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access - request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification - ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure - request deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction - ask us to restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Portability - receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object - object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent - at any time, for consent-based processing, without affecting lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any right, email hello@geofictionlabs.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential session cookies set by Supabase to maintain your anonymous session across page refreshes. These are strictly necessary and cannot be opted out of while using the service. We do not use any tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. No third-party cookies are set by this application.

9. Children

MapTheMovie is intended for players aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are the parent or guardian of a child who has provided personal data without your consent, please contact us at hello@geofictionlabs.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. International Transfers

Your data is stored in Supabase's EU-region infrastructure. If any transfer outside the UK or EEA is necessary, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (such as UK IDTA or EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use after notice of a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
